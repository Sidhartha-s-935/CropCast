CROPCAST

Welcome to CropCast, a tool designed to help you understand and analyze crop production and yield data specific to your region. Whether you’re a farmer, researcher, or simply interested in agricultural trends, CropCast provides valuable insights into crop performance. Explore the data, compare crops, and discover which ones are most efficient in terms of both production and yield. This tool is here to assist you in making informed decisions based on real, regional agricultural data.

HOW TO USE

  • Select a state and district to view production and yield data specific to that region.
  • Use the visualizations to compare crops, their efficiency, and how well they perform in terms of both total production and yield.

UNDERSTANDING DATA

  • Production refers to the total amount of crop harvested from a given area (e.g., tons per 1,000 acres).
  • Yield refers to the crop's productivity, measured by the amount harvested per unit of land .
The predictions and recommendations made by CropCast are not guaranteed and should be used as a guideline.
